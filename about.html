About Rotaract Bagalore East

Rotaract Bangalore East is a community-based club from Bangalore, established on 17 January 1975 and rechartered in July 2019.

We are a group of bright, energetic and motivated youngsters creating a positive change in society.

At Rotaract Bangalore East we believe in 3 core values "UNITE . RISE . EMPOWER":
UNITE: We are a family and family means no one gets left behind or forgotten.
RISE: With Unity, we RISE by learning from each other.
EMPOWER: With Unity, we Rise and Empower the Community around us.

Be part of the journey by Volunteering, Collaborating and Supporting the Events and Projects. Let's experience the joy of giving back to the community.
