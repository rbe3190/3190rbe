We are Rotaractors

Rotaract Bangalore East is a community-based club from Bangalore, sponsored by Rotary Bangalore East. We are a group of bright, energetic and motivated youngsters creating a positive change in society.

Through our activities, we aim to create a positive social impact and experience the joy of giving back to the community. Be part of the journey by Volunteering, Collaborating and Supporting the Events and Projects. Let's "UNITE . RISE . EMPOWER".
